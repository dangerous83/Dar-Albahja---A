٠٢ / 02Brand Story

The House of Joy

دار البهجة — بيت الفرح والضيافة

“Dar” means home. “Albahja” means joy — and it is also the beloved nickname of Marrakech itself: the City of Joy.

This identity is built on that single powerful idea: every guest who walks in is not entering a restaurant — they are entering a Moroccan home where food, warmth and celebration are shared. Nothing in this logo is decorative by accident; every line carries the heritage of Marrakech.

Moroccan inspiration الإلهام المغربي

Horseshoe arches, zellige geometry, arabesque foliage and the tagine — studied and drawn by hand before any digital work began.

Core elements العناصر الأساسية

The ornamental frame (symmetry & protection), the tagine icon (heritage & gathering), rising steam (warmth & life) and the floral star (beauty & tradition).

٣

Maroon for heritage and pride, green for nature and harmony, gold for warmth and luxury — drawn from Morocco's landscapes and tiles.
